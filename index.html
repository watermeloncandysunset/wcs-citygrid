About

Citygrid is a solitaire-type game that was originally published in the The Board Game Remix Kit ⧉, using Monopoly deeds as cards.

Since the closing of the original publisher, the BGRK has been released for free under the CC-BY SA License ⧉.

This browser version was coded by Watermelon Candy Sunset to make this specific game easier to play digitally, but also mostly for fun.

As with the original, this version also uses the CC-BY SA License.

How to Play

Each turn, you place the card on the top of the deck onto the grid.

You can only place cards in a cell that is adjacent to an already-filled cell.

Houses () must be adjacent to another House of the same color.

Power Stations () must be a placed in a cell with the power icon.

Train Stations () must be either in the same row or column as another Train Station, but not adjacent to a Train Station.

These conditions are checked at the end of the game, so don't worry if a neighborhood is temporarily disconnected.

As long as they are connected at the end, when all the cells are filled, all is good.
